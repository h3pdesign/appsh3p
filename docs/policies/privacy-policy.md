# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: February 17, 2026_

This Privacy Policy explains how data is handled on https://apps-h3p.com.

## 1. Data collection

This documentation site is designed to minimize data collection.

- No user account is required.
- No first-party advertising trackers are intentionally installed.
- No payment data is processed directly on this site.

## 2. Technical logs

Hosting providers and CDNs may process technical request data (for example IP address, user agent, timestamps, requested URL) for security, delivery, and reliability.

## 3. Cookies and local storage

The site uses limited local browser storage for UI preferences (for example visual effect toggle) and may use cookies required by hosting or third-party embedded services.

See [EU Cookie Notice](/policies/eu-cookie-notice).

## 4. Third-party services

Outbound links to services such as GitHub, Apple App Store, TestFlight, Patreon, and social platforms are governed by those services' own privacy policies.

## 5. Data retention

Where technical logs are processed by infrastructure providers, retention is controlled by those providers and their security policies.

## 6. Your rights (EU/EEA)

If applicable, you may request access, correction, deletion, restriction, objection, and portability rights under GDPR, subject to legal limitations.

## 7. Contact

For privacy requests, use [Support and Feedback](/support/support-and-feedback).
