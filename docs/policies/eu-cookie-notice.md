# EU Cookie Notice

_Last updated: February 17, 2026_

This notice explains cookie and local-storage usage for visitors in the EU/EEA.

## 1. Essential functionality

The docs site may store minimal browser data required for core functionality and UI preferences.

Examples:

- visual preference flags (for example effects toggle)
- technical state needed for navigation behavior

## 2. Analytics and marketing cookies

At this time, no first-party behavioral ad tracking is intentionally enabled by this documentation site.

## 3. Third-party cookies

When you follow external links (GitHub, Apple, Patreon, social platforms), those third parties may set cookies under their own policies.

## 4. Managing cookies

You can delete or block cookies/local storage in your browser settings.

Disabling essential storage may reduce some site functionality.
